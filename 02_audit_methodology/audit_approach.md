# Internal Audit Approach for DORA

## Audit Philosophy
The audit approach is risk-based, independent and aligned
with the Three Lines of Defence model.

## Objectives
- Assess compliance with DORA requirements
- Evaluate effectiveness of ICT controls
- Identify material ICT-related risks

## Audit Principles
- Risk-based scoping
- Evidence-driven assessment
- Proportionality based on institution size and complexity
