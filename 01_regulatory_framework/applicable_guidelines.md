# Applicable Guidelines and Standards

## European Guidelines
- EBA Guidelines on ICT and Security Risk Management
- EBA Guidelines on Outsourcing Arrangements

## National Guidance
- BaFin Supervisory Communications on DORA
- Deutsche Bundesbank IT supervision publications

## International Standards (Best Practice)
- ISO/IEC 27001 – Information Security Management
- ISO 22301 – Business Continuity Management

## Audit Use
These guidelines serve as interpretative support
where DORA requirements are principle-based.
